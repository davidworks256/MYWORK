Challenges in Uganda

Lack of Access:
Over 24 million people lack basic drinking water; 29 million lack improved sanitation.
Health Impact:
Diarrhea kills over 4,500 children under five annually.
Educational Impact:
Girls miss school due to lack of sanitation facilities.
Geographical Challenges:
40% of households cite long distances to water sources.
Hygiene Behavior:
Handwashing and hygiene education are essential for lasting change.
